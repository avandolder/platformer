list of things items can do:
incPH arg1 - increment the player's health by arg1 amount when used
incMaxPH arg1 - increment the player's max health by arg1 amount when used or equipped
incDef arg1 - increment defense when equiped by arg1 amount
incAtk arg1 - increment attack when equipped by arg1 amount
isGrapplingHook - says whether the item is a grappling hook or not
isShot - says whether the item is shot or not
shotImage arg1 - image of when the item is shot (16*16 for 2 directions)
shotDamage arg1 - damage done by the shot item
shotRate arg1 - amount of shots allowed on the screen at once
canDoubleJump - allows the player to double jump
